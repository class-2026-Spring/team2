Headache (두통)
ℹ️ Check the precautions and information below. If symptoms persist, visit the nearest clinic.
About
A common symptom that can range from a dull ache to sharp pain. Staying hydrated and resting in a quiet, dark room often helps relieve mild tension headaches.
Precautions
✓ Rest in a quiet, well-ventilated room.
✓ Drink plenty of warm water to stay hydrated.
Available OTC Drugs
You can buy these non-prescription medicines at general pharmacies in Korea without a prescription.
Acetaminophen (타이레놀)
Effective for general headaches and fever reducers. Suitable for empty stomachs.
⚠️ Do not take with alcohol (liver warning).
Emergency Warning Signs
• Sudden, extremely severe headache ("thunderclap").
• Headache accompanied by numbness, slurred speech, or confusion.
